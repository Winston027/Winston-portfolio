内容与思考 Notes Notes on Content

不只是“写几句好看的文案”，而是把内容当作产品的一部分来设计。 Beyond “nice copy”. Treat content as part of the product, not decoration on top.

当一个按钮变成一句话：内容设计的介入时机 When a Button Becomes a Sentence: Timing Content Design

为什么很多产品到最后才想起“找个人来写写文案”？内容如果从一开始就参与信息架构和任务拆解，产品会长得完全不一样。 Many teams add “copy” at the very end. If content joins from the moment we shape IA and tasks, the product grows into something else entirely.

长文 · 结构化碎念 Long-form · Structured Notes

品牌语气不等于“多用感叹号” Brand Voice Is Not “More Exclamation Marks”

语气不是“加三个感叹号”和“全句拟人化”，而是你愿意在哪里说“不知道”，在哪里坦白你的限制。 Voice is not about more exclamation marks or constant personification. It’s about where you say “we don’t know yet” and how you admit your limits.

信念与偏好 Beliefs & Preferences

  • 好的内容不是“更会说话”，而是“更懂什么时候闭嘴”。 Good content is not “talks more”, it “knows when to stay quiet”.
  • 任何一个“下一步”按钮都值得被认真对待。 Every “Next” button deserves to be taken seriously.
  • 语气可以有个性，但不应该牺牲清晰度。 Voice can have personality, but not at the cost of clarity.
  • 所有“用户教育”，本质上都是“你有没有耐心解释世界”。 All “user education” is essentially: do you have the patience to explain the world?
